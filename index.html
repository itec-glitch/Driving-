Before You Start

  • 1

    Adjust Your Seat

    Position yourself so you can reach all pedals comfortably while maintaining a slight bend in your knees.

  • 2

    Adjust Mirrors

    Set your rearview and side mirrors to minimize blind spots. You should see the road behind, not your own vehicle.

  • 3

    Fasten Seatbelt

    Always buckle up before starting the engine. Seatbelts reduce the risk of fatal injury by 45%.

  • 4

    Check Surroundings

    Walk around your vehicle before driving. Check for obstacles, children, or pets that may be hidden from view.
